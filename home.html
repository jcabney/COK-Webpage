What To Expect

We aren't the flashiest or most professional church, but we don't want to be. We aren't the biggest church, but we're fine with that. We are intentionally being exactly what God has called us to be: real people, who live and work in Columbia, who love Jesus.

King's Kids

Children are welcome in our service. During the sermon, a special class is held for children ages 3-9. We also have an infant nursery available.

Worship

Worship is the most important and meaningful thing we do together as a church. Our worship is both ancient and modern. We gather each week to pray, sing, read scripture, hear a sermon, and celebrate the Lord's Supper.

Community

We love to party! Throughout the year there are always picnics, parties, and other gatherings for you to experience community. We also have small groups near your neighborhood that meet twice a month for Bible Study, prayer, and fellowship.

Care

We are committed to close-knit community, pastoral care, Christ-centered worship, Biblical preaching, serving our city, expressing our unity with all Christian believers, and celebrating all good things with great joy.
